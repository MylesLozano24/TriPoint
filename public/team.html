Meet the founders

Two engineers, one working system.

TriPoint Innovations is led by two Oklahoma State University engineering students combining technical development, business strategy, and hands-on product execution.

Myles & Reese — prototype photo placeholder
Co-Founder

Myles Lozano

Myles Lozano is a mechanical and aerospace engineering student at Oklahoma State University with experience in engineering design, product development, aerospace systems, prototyping, business development, and technical leadership.

At TriPoint Innovations, Myles contributes to company strategy, hardware development, product design, partnerships, customer discovery, and commercialization efforts. His experience across engineering internships, entrepreneurship programs, and leadership roles supports TriPoint's mission of moving ambitious concepts toward practical products.

Co-Founder

Reese Huhnke

Reese Huhnke is an Oklahoma State University engineering student focused on technical development, product design, software, prototyping, and problem solving.

At TriPoint Innovations, Reese contributes to the development of Hunter Radar's hardware and software systems, including device communication, interface development, testing, and product refinement. His technical approach helps translate the company's ideas into working systems that can be tested and improved.
