Meet the Founders

Two engineers, one working system.

Myles Lozano and Reese Huhnke are the co-founders of TriPoint Innovations. Together, they combine engineering experience, product development, business strategy, and startup leadership to advance Hunter Radar from an early concept into a working technology platform.

Myles & Reese — prototype photo placeholder
Co-Founder

Myles Lozano

Myles is pursuing dual degrees in Mechanical Engineering and Aerospace Engineering at Oklahoma State University, along with a minor in Electrical Engineering. He leads the majority of TriPoint's product development and day-to-day execution, including hardware design, prototyping, technical strategy, testing, partnerships, and the continued development of Hunter Radar.

His engineering experience includes internships with SRC, GE Aerospace, and the Oklahoma State University New Product Development Center, where he has worked across product development, aerospace manufacturing, prototyping, engineering analysis, and technical problem-solving.

Myles was also recognized as an Oklahoma State University Top 20 Freshman.

Field-testing photo placeholder
Co-Founder

Reese Huhnke

Reese is pursuing a degree in Chemical Engineering at Oklahoma State University. He focuses on TriPoint's financial planning, marketing, business development, branding, customer discovery, and commercialization efforts.

His professional experience includes internships with the Oklahoma State University New Product Development Center and ParaNano, a biotechnology startup, where he gained experience in product development, startup operations, technical innovation, and early-stage company growth.

Reese was also recognized as an Oklahoma State University Top 20 Freshman.

Together, Myles and Reese have advanced TriPoint Innovations through the Oklahoma Farm Bureau Activate Accelerator, the Riata Startup Experience, a third-place hackathon finish, prototype development, customer discovery, and provisional patent protection for Hunter Radar.
