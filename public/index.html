Introducing Hunter Radar

Location awareness where cellular service ends.

Hunter Radar is an off-grid outdoor safety and communication system for hunters, landowners, hikers, guides, and outdoor groups. It combines portable hardware with a digital map interface so group members stay aware of one another across large or remote properties — without depending entirely on cell towers.

  • Group location sharing
  • Offline communication
  • Last-known location tracking
  • Short status updates
  • Emergency alerts
  • Property boundaries
